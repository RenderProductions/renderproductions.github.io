What makes it different

Built with intention

⚔

Real consequences in combat

Every action costs turns. Heavy attacks telegraph before they land. Stun, bleed, poison, paralysis, corrode — 18 status effects that interact and stack. A bad fight can spiral. A clever one can end in two moves.

📖

A story that tracks everything

NPCs remember. Flags persist across deaths. Take too long to find James Alfrey and you miss him forever. Save Chuck before the chain, or watch it play out. The world does not reset to wait for you.

💀

Death is not the end

When you die, Azrael shows you your stats and what killed you — then sends you to Wonderland. A place you can explore, speak to the ghosts of characters from previous runs, and maybe find a way back.

🏆

Achievements that mean something

Most unlock unique weapons, cosmetics, or secret content. The Trifecta of Saviors requires 100% completion on Chaotic difficulty without dying once. The rewards are worth it.

🎮

Playable in Discord

Built as a Discord Embedded Activity. Play directly in a voice channel — your friends can see who's in the session, and your status updates as you fight, explore, and die.
