## What is Varandia: Retold?
 
Varandia: Retold is an indie RPG in active development, playable as a **Discord Embedded Activity**. It features:
 
- ⚔️ **Turn-based combat** with 18 status effects, parry, brace, and heavy attack telegraphing
- 📖 **A story that tracks everything** — NPCs remember your actions across runs
- 💀 **Death isn't the end** — when you die, you're sent to Wonderland
- 🏆 **60+ achievements** that unlock weapons, cosmetics, and secret content
- 🎮 **Playable in Discord** — friends can see who's in your session in real time
